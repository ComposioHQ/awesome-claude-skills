## Notion:find
**Category:** Notion Integration

**What it does:**
Quickly finds pages or databases in Notion by title keywords. Faster than full search for when you know (roughly) what you're looking for.

**When to trigger:**
- "Find the Notion page called X"
- "Look up [title] in Notion"

**How to install:**
```bash
npx claude install superpowers
```
Also requires the **Notion MCP server** connected.

**Trigger phrase:** Ask Claude to find a specific Notion page by name.
